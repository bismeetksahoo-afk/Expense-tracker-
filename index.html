How to Use on Mobile
1
Save to Home Screen
iOS: Share - Add to Home Screen
Android: Menu - Add to Home Screen
2
Copy your bank SMS
Long-press the SMS alert and tap Copy
3
Open app, tap Add tab
Long-press the dark box, tap Paste, then tap Auto-fill
4
Verify and Save
Check the parsed fields and tap Save Expense
